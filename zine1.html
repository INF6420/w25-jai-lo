The TooLongDidntRead Companion for SEEDS AS DEEP TIME TECHNOLOGIES by Caylen Cole-Hazel


Caylen is a good friend of mine and shared this zine with me last week. Instead of creating a powerpoint presenations to recap this article they read for a group discussion, Caylen made a zine. I love the idea of rethinking digital (or immaterial) recaps and giving people the opportunity to flip through this condensed and artistic summary.
